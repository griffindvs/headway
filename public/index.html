How We Work

We are a network of high school students in Austin, Texas, bound by a common goal of helping our peers to find academic success.

Our goal is to provide educational aid to all students, including those who cannot afford it. For this reason, we offer tutoring for the SAT, ACT, PSAT, SAT Subject Tests, and a variety of specific classes free of charge. We also offer our services both in person in South Austin, Texas, as well as online through video and text chat platforms.
